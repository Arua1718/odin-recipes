Cookies

Description

This is for making cookies to eat.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Eggs
  • Butter
  • Sugar
  • Baking Soda
  • Vanilla Extract
  • Salt
  • Milk
  • Chocolate Chips

Steps

  1. Sift Dry Ingrediants
  2. Cream Butter and Sugar
  3. Beat in Eggs
  4. Add Det Ingrediants to Wet
  5. Fold in Chips
  6. Shape Cookies
  7. Bake and Cool
